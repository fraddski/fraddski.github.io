attraction

East Side Gallery

location Mühlenstraße 1, 10243 Berlin Friedrichshain

website www.eastsidegallery-berlin.de

The Kunstmeile, or art mile, is located along the banks of the river Spree in Friedrichshain. At 1316m long it is the longest open air gallery in the world as well as the longest segment of the Berlin Wall that is still standing.

attraction

Fat Tire Bike Tours

location Panorama Str 1a 10178 Berlin

times All-in-One City Bike Tour leaves daily at 11am & 4pm

website fattirebiketours.com/berlin

information Tours meet at the main entrance of the TV Tower in Alexanderplatz.

Covering 10km over 4.5 hours, the all-in-one city bike tour provides a perfect orientation to Berlin.

Highlights include Checkpoint Charlie, the Berlin Wall, Hitler's bunker, the Brandenburg Gate, Reichstag, Museum Island, the Memorial to the Murdered Jews of Europe, Bebelplatz and more.

Stops for lunch about 2/3 through the tour at a beer garden in the heart of the city park.
