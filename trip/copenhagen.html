attraction

Rundetaarn

location Købmagergade 52A, 1150 Copenhagen

times 10am to 8pm

website www.rundetaarn.dk

The 17th century Round Tower is the oldest functioning observatory in Europe. The outdoor platform encircling the observatory has a magnificent view of the old part of Copenhagen, and is at the top of a spiral walk approximately 209m long.

restaurant

Den Økologiske Pølsemand

location Next to the Round Tower

times 11am to 6pm

website doep.dk

Once voted the favourite Copenhagen eatery, Den Økologiske Pølsemand (the organic hotdog man), or Døp for short, can be found next to the Round Tower serving up organic sausages in buns made from slow-fermented sourdough bread and linseed.
